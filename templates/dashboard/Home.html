Built with Python

World of Trajectories is a free trajectory data processing and visualization websites built with Python and Django. It was created based on MovingPandas and PyMove

You can analyze all kinds of trajectory data by using this tools. For example, you can predict the trend of extreme weather like hurricane; you can study migration routes of animals; you can also evaluate the traffic situation of city.

Bowhead Whale

The bowhead whale is a species of baleen whale belonging to the family Balaenidae and the only living representative of the genus Balaena. They are the only baleen whale endemic to the Arctic and subarctic waters, and are named after their characteristic massive triangular skull, which they use to break through Arctic ice.

African Elephant

The African elephant is a genus comprising two living elephant species, the African bush elephant and the smaller African forest elephant. Both are social herbivores with grey skin, but differ in the size and colour of their tusks and in the shape and size of their ears and skulls.
